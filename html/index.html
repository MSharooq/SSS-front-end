ABOUT SEDS CUSAT

IRES-SEDS CUSAT is a community of undergraduate students from CUSAT who have come together to make opportunities out of the possibilities that are beyond the perimeters of earth. We aim to take knowledge down the path of imagination. To uphold this vision and idea of ours we come together as a community to administer seminars, forums, and outreach programs to break you from the monotonous way of learning. We present the battle of minds both at the national and international level for college and school students and assist them in networking within space industries. Here memories are created with dreams not dependent on the stars falling in the night sky. We bring to you our unconference SPACEUP CUSAT 2023.
